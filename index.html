    Getting Started

    Welcome to Squardle, a Wordle-type game!

    You have 15 guesses to find all 5 hidden words. Guesses are submitted in the top row. In the example below, 'STARE' would be the first guess.

    Words are displayed in each row. In the example below, 'C' and 'R' have been guessed in the correct position. 'E', however, is in the word but its correct position has not been found.

    Unlike Wordle, you can play as much! Refresh the page to get a new batch of words. Good luck and have fun!
